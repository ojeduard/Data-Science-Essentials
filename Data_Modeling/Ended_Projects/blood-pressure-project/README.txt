Data Source:

US Data:
National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) 2017-2018 collected by the Data Challenge Lab Open Content (https://github.com/dcl-docs/dcldata). From this dataset, we sampled 100 individuals randomly.

Yanomami Data:
Mueller, Noel T., et al. "Association of age with blood pressure across the lifespan in isolated Yanomami and Yekwana villages." JAMA cardiology 3.12 (2018): 1247-1249.
